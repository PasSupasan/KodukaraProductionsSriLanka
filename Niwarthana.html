Niwarthana

About the Song

Niwarthana is an original musical creation by Kodukara Productions that explores themes of emotional reflection, personal growth, and inner transformation. The song blends expressive lyrics with a gentle melodic structure, allowing listeners to connect deeply with its message. Through thoughtful composition and visual storytelling, Niwarthana presents a calm yet powerful artistic experience that reflects contemporary Sri Lankan music creativity.
